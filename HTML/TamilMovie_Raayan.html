MELODIFY - RAAYAN MOVIE

About

Raayan is a 2024 Indian Tamil-language neo-noir action drama film written and directed by Dhanush, in his second directorial venture. It is produced by Kalanithi Maran under Sun Pictures. The film stars Dhanush in the titular role, alongside Sundeep Kishan, Kalidas Jayaram, Dushara Vijayan, S. J. Suryah, Selvaraghavan, Prakash Raj, Aparna Balamurali, Varalaxmi Sarathkumar, Saravanan, Dileepan and Divya Pillai. In the film, a fast-food hotel owner in North Chennai struggles to protect his family when they inadvertently get muddled in a war between two rival gangs resulting in unforeseen circumstances.
